## Architecture Reminders

- `masterVolume` param is in dB (-60 to +6) — do NOT call `gainToDecibels()` on it
- `IS_SYNTH FALSE`, `VST3_CATEGORIES "Fx"` — Grano is an audio effect in hosts
- `Knob::valueLabel_`: `juce::String(v, 2)` fixed precision; span-based formula unreliable (NormalisableRange skew affects `getRange()`)
- WSL2 clock skew: must `rm -f CMakeFiles/Grano.dir/Source/XXX.cpp.o` before rebuilding to force recompile
