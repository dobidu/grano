## Important Context

- **LOAD button** lives in the header, left of LOOP. Calls `openFileChooser(0)`. S0-S3 buttons also open file chooser for their respective slots — they're not just slot selectors.
- **Drag-drop on Windows** uses WM_DROPFILES fallback (not OLE) because many DAWs init COM with COINIT_MULTITHREADED. `tryRegisterWindowDrop()` is idempotent, retried at 10 Hz until peer exists.
- **Pitch at +24st** was crashing because pitchRatio=4 pushed read position 4× past the +2 guard samples. Fix: early-return `true` when `srcInt > sourceLen`. Guard samples at `[sourceLen]` and `[sourceLen+1]` are still safe to read when within bounds.
- **masterVolume** param is already in dB (range -60..+6). Never call `gainToDecibels()` on it.
